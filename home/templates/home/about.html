About

Where it all began

We’re a friendly bike shop in Manchester. We’re grown over the years from servicing the postman’s bike to helping hundreds of you get on your bikes during lockdown.

Our members are a supportive bunch and we regularly head out on rides around Manchester together. We’d love you to come and join the community.

We’re excited to announce you’re now able to book our services online, as well as over the phone or by popping in to our workshop.

Services

Leo’s is here for all types of cyclists. Whether you’ve dug out an old bike from the shed, you’ve started commuting on your bike or you’re a seasoned pro, we’ve got a range of services to meet your needs.

If you’ve got a specialist bike, such as a tandem or e-bike, we know that you’ll need specialist services. Get in touch with us to discuss your needs and we’ll give you a bespoke quote.

Where do I start?

  • Sign up to become a member or log in
  • Add your bike to your profile and tell us about it
  • Take a look at our services and pick one that’s right for you
  • Book us in and we’ll come to you
  • You can log in and update your bike any time
